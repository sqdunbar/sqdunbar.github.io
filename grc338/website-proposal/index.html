Introduction

La Fleur Bakery

La Fluer Bakery is a fictitious bakery located in San Luis Obispo, California. Our mission is to deliver seasonal creative food, using the highest quality of ingredients + using traditional hand made methods. We are committed to working as a team to care for everyone who walks through our doors to contribute to the betterment of our community.

Target audience

The target audeince is milenials and gen-z who take aesthetic into account when making choices of where to go + what to buy. Not only are our baked goods delicious, but our enviroment is beautiful.

Our guest's primary task + goals are to order baked goods through our website as well as navigate them to come into our physical store-front.
